INTRODUCTION

Our Country is facing today a vital cultural breakdown which is the outcome of invasion of western culture through electronic media. Kids and adolescents are the worst affected of it, since they are the soft and very easily flexible and adoptable to changes- either proper, or improper. Educational standard of our State, in particular, is not reflecting respectable heights. Success ratio of M.P. in national level exams- may it be civil or technical - is not so high. Certainly, there is no dearth of talent in the State, but what cut their success down are lack of proper environment, guidance and poverty. In big cities, there are certain institutes imparting good education, but they are out of reach of the poor people. In this context, the NEW HUMANISTIC EDUCATIONAL SOCIETY, MADHYA PRADESH was registered under madhya pradesh societies Registration Act 1973. The Society will be able to serve the people all over M.P. This institute is an off-shoot of the society.
The institute Bright Career Special Academy, Sagar aims at imparting high-Standard of education, inculcating love for our perennial cultural values to kids, and adolescents. A blending of oriental cultural, and occidental scientific outlook will reflect through the young ones, stepping into the institute. their all round development will be ensured through intensive academic sessions and extensive extra-curricular activities, like participation in N.C.C., Scout, Games and Sports, Painting, music, Seminars, Debates, Excursions etc. Fee structure is affordable, so as not to tax heavily upon the financially weaker people, probably constituting a larger section of our society. ADDMISSION

Admission will be made against the availability of seats. The number of seats available in each class will be announced in the beginning of every session. Applicants interested to have admission in any class shall have to go through an entrance exam. The entrance exam will be conducted by the institute on a scheduled date, which will be qualifying the entrance test will have to submit the relevant certificates such as Transfer Certificate, and Birth Certificate, etc. However, decision of the Principal / Director of the institute regarding admission shall stand final.
N.B.: Entrance fee and Admission fee are non-refundable.

CONTACT WITH PARENTS

Close and happy co-operation between parents / guardians and school-management (Principal and teachers) is necessary for the all-round development of students.
There will be at least two guardians meeting, stretching over the span of one session. The date, timings and venue will be intimated to the parents / guardians at least 10 days in advance. They are requested to attend the meeting in the interest of childrens progress.
